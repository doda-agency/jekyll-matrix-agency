Our Mission

DoDa e-Agency is an Open Source AI-native agency designed to coordinate multiple artificial intelligence agents to help users perform online tasks efficiently and intuitively.

Our mission is to revolutionize human-machine interaction through an artificial intelligence-based interface that understands users' goals and autonomously completes tasks. We believe that the future of technology lies in eliminating complexity, allowing people to focus on what truly matters.

Our Vision

We envision a future where interaction with technology is completely natural and intuitive. DoDa e-Agency represents the first step towards this future, offering a simplified user experience through natural language processing and an advanced action model.

Our goal is to create an ecosystem where users can delegate complex tasks to an intelligent system that understands their intentions and acts accordingly, eliminating the need to navigate between multiple applications and interfaces.

Our Core Values

Open Source

We believe in the power of collaborative development and transparent technology. Our commitment to open source ensures that our solutions are accessible, customizable, and continuously improved by a global community.

Privacy

We prioritize user privacy and data security in all our solutions. Our technology is designed with privacy-by-design principles, ensuring that users maintain control over their data.

Accessibility

We are committed to creating technology that is accessible to everyone, regardless of their technical expertise or abilities. Our interfaces are designed to be intuitive and inclusive.

Our Team

DoDa e-Agency is powered by a diverse team of experts in artificial intelligence, software development, user experience design, and business strategy.
